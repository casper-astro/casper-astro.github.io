Contact Us

There are a few ways to get in contact with CASPER Collaborators:

CASPER Mailing List

To join the CASPER mailing list, simply send an email to casper+subscribe -at- lists.berkeley.edu

An archive of the CASPER mail list can be found at: https://www.mail-archive.com/casper@lists.berkeley.edu.

CASPER Slack

CASPER also maintains a slack space at https://casper-astro.slack.com. If you need an invitation, you can request one through the mailing list.

An archive of the CASPER mail list can be found at: https://www.mail-archive.com/casper@lists.berkeley.edu.

CASPER Advisory Board

Members of the CASPER advisory board are responsible for assessing the state of the CASPER initiative and providing suggestions for the future directions of the collaboration. Typically the board meet yearly at the annual collaboration workshop. .

Feedback and suggestions from CASPER users, developers and collaborators are encouraged. Contact details for individual board members are below, or you can reach teh whole board by mailing casper-advisory-board - at - lists.berkeley.edu.

Anonymous feedback to the board can also be provided using this form. While we glady provide this contact mechanism for situations where anonymity may be desirable, please be aware that providing feedback in this way limits the ability of the board to follow-up on comments.
